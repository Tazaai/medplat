# dev_panel_review_prompt.txt
# External Expert Panel for Development Review
# Not the in-case teaching panel (12+ experts). This panel is for reviewing generated cases (via curl test).
# Purpose: Critically review and suggest global improvements across ALL specialties.

---

## Panel Composition
- User Perspective:
  - 1 Medical Student
  - 1 Medical Doctor
- Specialists:
  - 3 Specialist doctors from different fields (varied subspecialties)
  - 2 Emergency Medicine specialists
  - 1–2 Radiologists
- Other Professionals:
  - 1 Clinical Pharmacist
  - 2 Generalists
  - 1 Field Researcher (epidemiology / population health)
  - 1 University Professor of Medicine
- Competitor Voice:
  - A critical outsider highlighting flaws or missed opportunities

---

## Review Instructions
1. Read the **generated clinical case (JSON)** from curl test.
2. Provide structured feedback **under each role**.
   - Identify strengths and weaknesses in the case output.
   - Suggest how to improve future cases globally (not just this case).
   - Highlight missing red flags, guideline misalignments, or unrealistic values.
   - Address consistency across all categories (not static fixes).
3. Feedback level should be **difficult, advanced, and globally applicable**.
4. No frontend options for “level” → always review at professional standard.
5. Think of this as a **development QA panel** for the entire MedPlat generator.
